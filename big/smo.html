SMO


For any website to reach its full potential, its important to optimize its reach over social media platforms as well. This is called social media optimization.

Social media optimization can help you to strengthen your brand, generate leads, gain an online presence, and to further connect with your audience/prospective clients. There are several aspects to social media optimization.

One such factor is doing keyword research. This means figuring out what your audience is searching for, and creating relevant content to that audience. This will also help you figure out what hashtags to use on social media, so that you can better connect with your audience.

Another large factor is optimizing your social media profiles. This can be accomplished by setting your account name as your business name. Also, having a direct, clear description of what your company does in your bio. Setting your profile photo as your company logo also helps. It also helps to have original, curated content on your social media, to help cater to your audience.

If you want to repost older, evergreen content(content that won’t become irrelevant), this can be a great tactic to grow your audience. But try to change it up so that your existing users don’t get bored. One way to do this is to change the headline of the social media post when you post it.

You can also install plugins to your website that will help your smo. One such plugin is “Digg Digg”, which will add sharing buttons next to your content for the top 20 social networking sites. This will allow other people to share your content for you, and grow your audience.

Wbiya Toolbar is another pluigin that allowsx readers to share and bookmark your content with a toolbar that is displayed at the button of your site, and is easily customizable.

Your website is important. And if you want other people to realize that, you have to let them find it. That’s why SMO is important for any website.
