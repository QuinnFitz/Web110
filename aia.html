Audience Issues Approach
Prospective fans, normally people 16-35 years old, basic computer skills, who love grunge, or rock music, and are not very religious.
  • Looking for good grunge music
  • They want to hear something different/original
  • Looking to discover an up and coming artist, so that they seem cool
  • Make it clear that Luke is a grunge musician.
  • Provide examples of Luke's music on the website, along with testimonials from others that reference his unique sound.
  • We mention on the website that Luke is an up and coming musician.
Active fans/subscribers, normally people 16-35, basic computer skills, who love grunge music, are not super religious
  • Finding a large portfolio of Luke's music.
  • Knowing when Luke releases new content.
  • Knowing when Luke's upcoming shows are happening.
  • Providing a large, organized catalogue of Luke's music on the website.
  • Setting up an email campaign, with an email signup form on the website, so that people can be notified when new content is released.
  • Have a page dedicated to upcoming show dates and locations, as well as send people notifications via the aforementioned email campaign.
Prospective Bookers
  • Contacting Luke
  • Knowing Luke's rates
  • Knowing when Luke is available
  • Have a seperate "Booking page" with its own contact form and other booking info.
  • List Luke's rates on the Booking page, or let Luke communicate it to interested bookers via email.
  • Having a calendar with highlighted days showing availability on afforementioned booking page.
